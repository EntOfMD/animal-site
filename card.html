Golden Snub-Nosed Monkey

Adapted to cold by having a short snub nose, so it won't get frost bitten

  • Binomial Name: Rhinopithecus roxellana
  • Natural Habitat: Mountains in China
  • Prefered Food: Lichens
  • Conservation Status: Endangered

Golden Snub-Nosed Monkeys live in treetops of Chinese mountains. Their natural predators include mammals: Wolfs, Dholes, Leopards, and birds including Golden Eagles. They are endangered from being hunted by the rich, and the government and are made into fur garments to cure rheumatism by their ancient beliefs.
